📝 *Put Option Selection Guide*

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📋 *Field Reference*

• `rate_of_return` — Annualised premium yield. `(mid price / strike) ÷ weeks to expiry × 52`. Good: ≥ 0.25 (no upper cap — higher return = more reward, soft-capped in the score at 80%)

• `sharpe_ratio` — Risk-adjusted return of the underlying stock. Higher = better reward per unit of risk. Good: > 1.0

• `strike_percentile` — Where the strike sits within the 20-day price range. **Diagnostic only — not used in scoring.** 0 = at the 20-day low, 1 = at the 20-day high.

• `strike_from` / `strike_to` — The max_drop band: the deep (safe) and shallow (risky) strike boundaries derived from the stock's historical drawdowns. A strike near `strike_from` is rarely breached; near `strike_to` is frequently breached.

• `trend_short` — Where current price sits relative to its 20-day EMA. > 1.0 = above trend, < 1.0 = below trend. Good: > 0.98

• `trend_long` — Where current price sits relative to its 50-day EMA. Captures intermediate trend direction. Good: > 0.98

• `score` — Composite quality score (0–1). Combines max_drop-band safety (40%), return quality (40%), Sharpe (20%). Options failing pre-filters are blank. Good: > 0.6

• `price_percentile` — Where current price sits within its 20-day range. Drives momentum flag: NORMAL (≤80%), HIGH (80–90%), EXTENDED (>90%). Good: ≤ 0.80

• `earnings_before_expiry` — Earnings date if it falls before option expiry. Empty = no earnings risk. Good: empty

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ *Quick Filter — 4 Rules*

1️⃣ `score > 0.6` — Primary gate. Bundles Sharpe, safety, and return checks.

2️⃣ `earnings_before_expiry` is empty — Earnings cause gap risk and IV crush. Avoid.

3️⃣ `price_percentile ≤ 0.80` — Stock trading at top of range = overextended. Stick to NORMAL momentum.

4️⃣ `rate_of_return` ≥ 0.25 — Below 0.25 not worth the capital. No upper limit: a high return is a reward, not a danger signal (danger comes from the max_drop band, reflected in the score).

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📊 *Score Breakdown*

🛡 Safety (max_drop band) — 40%
Strike near `strike_from` (deep, rare historical drop) = safer. Strike near `strike_to` (shallow, typical drop) = riskier.

💰 Return quality — 40%
Soft-cap at 80% annualised. Rewarded up to 80%; above 80% still accepted (no penalty, no exclusion).

📊 Sharpe ratio — 20%
Historical risk-adjusted return quality.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚩 *Red Flags — Avoid These*

❌ `score` is blank — Failed pre-filter: return below 0.25 or non-positive Sharpe.

❌ `earnings_before_expiry` has a date — Stock can gap well below your strike.

❌ `price_percentile > 0.90` (EXTENDED) — Stock at 20-day high. May overshoot before reverting.
