📝 *Put Option Selection Guide*

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📋 *Field Reference*

• `rate_of_return` — Annualised premium yield. `(mid price / strike) ÷ weeks to expiry × 52`. Good: 0.25–0.65

• `sharpe_ratio` — Risk-adjusted return of the underlying stock. Higher = better reward per unit of risk. Good: > 1.0

• `strike_percentile` — Where the strike sits within the stock's expected support range. 0 = deep support, 1 = at resistance. Good: ≤ 0.60

• `trend_short` — Where current price sits relative to its 20-day EMA. > 1.0 = above trend, < 1.0 = below trend. Good: > 0.98

• `trend_long` — Where current price sits relative to its 50-day EMA. Captures intermediate trend direction. Good: > 0.98

• `score` — Composite quality score (0–1). Combines Trend (30%), strike safety (30%), Sharpe (20%), return quality (20%). Options failing pre-filters are blank. Good: > 0.6

• `price_percentile` — Where current price sits within its 20-day range. Drives momentum flag: NORMAL (≤80%), HIGH (80–90%), EXTENDED (>90%). Good: ≤ 0.80

• `earnings_before_expiry` — Earnings date if it falls before option expiry. Empty = no earnings risk. Good: empty

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ *Quick Filter — 5 Rules*

1️⃣ `score > 0.6` — Primary gate. Bundles trend, Sharpe, safety, and return checks.

2️⃣ `earnings_before_expiry` is empty — Earnings cause gap risk and IV crush. Avoid.

3️⃣ `price_percentile ≤ 0.80` — Stock trading at top of range = overextended. Stick to NORMAL momentum.

4️⃣ `rate_of_return` between 0.25 and 0.65 — Below 0.25 not worth capital. Above 0.65 = market pricing in high risk.

5️⃣ `trend_short > 0.98 AND trend_long > 0.98` — Stocks below their moving average are in a downtrend. Selling puts on them exposes you to sustained declines.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📊 *Score Breakdown*

📈 Trend (EMA ratio) — 30%
Most forward-looking signal. Stocks above their EMAs tend to keep rising.

🛡 Safety (strike percentile) — 30%
Lower strike vs support = more room before you're at risk.

📊 Sharpe ratio — 20%
Historical risk-adjusted return quality.

💰 Return quality — 20%
Prefers ~35% annualised. Penalises extreme returns signalling danger.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚩 *Red Flags — Avoid These*

❌ `score` is blank — Failed pre-filter. Return too low/high, negative Sharpe, or strike above resistance.

❌ `earnings_before_expiry` has a date — Stock can gap well below your strike.

❌ `price_percentile > 0.90` (EXTENDED) — Stock at 20-day high. May overshoot before reverting.

❌ `rate_of_return > 0.65` — Market is pricing high probability of a drop. You're being paid for real risk.

❌ `trend_short < 0.98 or trend_long < 0.98` — Stock is below its moving average = downtrend. Selling puts on falling stocks catches falling knives.
